A word from the general manager

A sympathetic word to the General Manager

We are pleased to welcome the most beautiful welcome to visitors and surfers of the official website of the Civil Status and Passports Department. Emphasizing on the department's keenness to activate and develop the website in order to play its role towards keeping pace with everything that would clearly reflect our vision of continuous development in line with the royal directives of the government and urge it to continue the constant endeavor to improve the provision of the best services that meet the expectations of service recipients. Our goal in this site is to be in contact with you by informing you of all the services provided by the department. And all the information necessary to provide the service and receive complaints and suggestions, hoping by that to reduce the burden on all dealers with the department and provide them with means of comfort. In conclusion, I can only extend my heartfelt thanks to everyone who communicates with the department, either through a visit or contact with the department, or through the website, which we were keen to be an interactive portal through which we invite you to continuously communicate with us.



Fahad Al-Amoush
