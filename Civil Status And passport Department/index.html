Electronic Service

Launching an electronic service for issuing and renewing passports for Jordanians.

The Ministry of Interior, in cooperation with the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology and the Civil Status and Passports Department, launched a service for issuing and renewing passports for Jordanians electronically for the first time. The Minister of Interior affirmed that the launch of the service comes as a continuation and implementation of the government's approach derived from the directives of His Majesty King Abdullah II to develop the level of services provided to citizens and to provide all necessary means to simplify the procedures before them and facilitate them during the process of providing and receiving these services.
